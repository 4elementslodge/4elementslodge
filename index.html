Our Identity & Journey

The Meaning Behind Our Logo

When you look at the logo for 4 Elements Lodge, you will see a journey woven into symbols: a cross, a heartbeat, a heart, and a dove.

To us, this isn't just a design—it is the soul of our lodge. The cross represents our grounding and faith. The heartbeat and heart symbolize the vibrant life, love, and connection we share here today—anchored by my children, George and Leo, whose energy and involvement bring this beautiful space to life for our family, friends, and guests.

The dove carries a special, quiet weight. It flies in honor of the people we have lost—those precious souls who are no longer walking beside us, but whose presence we carry in our hearts forever.

4 Elements Lodge was built to be a peaceful retreat. It is a place to step away from the world, to remember, to heal, and to find quiet comfort. Whether you are here to celebrate life with loved ones or to find a moment of calm reflection, our doors—and our hearts—are open to you.

- The Dougias Family
